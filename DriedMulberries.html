Dried Mulberries

“Indulge in the sweet, tangy flavor of our premium dried mulberries. Sourced from top-quality producers and carefully dried to retain their natural flavor and nutrients, our mulberries are available in a variety of grades and varieties, including white and black.

Whether you’re looking for a healthy snack or an ingredient to add to your favorite recipes, our dried mulberries are the perfect choice. Packed with antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals, our mulberries are a delicious and nutritious addition to any diet.”

Grades and Varieties Available
  1. White mulberries – These are mulberries that are dried with their white color intact. They have a mild, sweet flavor and a chewy texture.
  2. Black mulberriess– These are mulberries that are dried with their dark color intact. They have a rich, sweet flavor and a slightly crunchy texture.
  3. Turkish mulberries – These are mulberries that are grown in Turkey and are known for their large size, sweetness, and delicate texture.
  4. Chinese mulberries –These are mulberries that are grown in China and are known for their smaller size and tangy, slightly tart flavor.

In terms of grades, some dried mulberry products may specify the grade of the fruit used. For example:

  1. Grade A – These are the highest quality mulberries, with a uniform size, color, and texture.
  2. Grade B – These mulberries may have some imperfections in terms of size, color, or texture, but are still considered good quality.
  3. Grade C – These are the lowest quality mulberries, with more imperfections and a less uniform appearance.

It’s worth noting that not all dried mulberries are graded, and that grading standards may vary depending on the producer. Regardless of the grade or variety, dried mulberries are a delicious and nutritious snack that can be enjoyed on their own or used in a variety of recipes.

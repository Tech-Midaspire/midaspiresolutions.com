Dried Cranberries

Home  >  Dried Cranberries
Home » products » Dried Cranberries

Our dried cranberries are a delicious and nutritious snack that can be enjoyed on their own or used in a variety of recipes.

They are made from high-quality, hand-picked cranberries that are dried to perfection to preserve their natural flavor and texture. Our dried cranberries are a rich source of antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals, making them a great addition to any diet.

Whether you are looking for a healthy snack or an ingredient to enhance your recipes, our dried cranberries are the perfect choice.

Grades and Varieties Available

Grades:

  1. Grade A – This is the highest quality grade of dry cranberries, with a uniform size, shape, and color. They are also free from defects and have a sweet and tart taste.
  2. Grade B – This grade of dry cranberries may have some minor defects such as discoloration or uneven sizes, but they are still suitable for use in baking and cooking.

Varieties:

  1. Sweetened – Sweetened dry cranberries are coated with sugar or other sweeteners to enhance their flavor and preserve their texture.
  2. Unsweetened– Unsweetened dry cranberries have no added sugars or sweeteners, and are a good option for those looking to limit their sugar intake.
  3. Organic –Organic dry cranberries are grown without the use of synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. They are also non-GMO and are considered a healthier alternative to conventionally grown cranberries.
  4. Conventional – Conventional dry cranberries are grown using synthetic pesticides and fertilizers. They are widely available and less expensive than organic cranberries.

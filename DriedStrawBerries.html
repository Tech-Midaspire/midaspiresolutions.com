Dried Strawberries

“Enjoy the sweet and tangy flavor of ripe strawberries all year round with our delicious dried strawberries. Made from high-quality, hand-picked strawberries, our dried strawberries are perfect for snacking or adding to your favorite recipes. Our strawberries are carefully dried to preserve their natural flavor and nutrients, and are available in whole or sliced varieties. Whether you’re looking for a healthy snack or a tasty ingredient for your next baking project, our dried strawberries are sure to satisfy your cravings.”

Grades and Varieties Available
  1. Whole dried strawberries – These are dried strawberries that are left whole, with the stems removed. They are often used as a snack or in baking.
  2. Sliced dried strawberries – These are dried strawberries that have been sliced into thin pieces. They are often used in baking, as a topping for yogurt or oatmeal, or as a snack.
  3. Sweetened dried strawberries – These are dried strawberries that have been coated in sugar or another sweetener. They are often used in baking or as a sweet snack.
  4. Unsweetened dried strawberries – These are dried strawberries that have not been coated in sugar or another sweetener. They are often used as a snack or in recipes that call for unsweetened dried fruit.

In terms of varieties , some dried strawberry products may specify the variety of strawberry used. For example, some products may use the Albion variety, which is known for its sweet flavor and firm texture. Other varieties commonly used for drying include Camarosa, Chandler, and Seascape.


The grade of dried strawberries can also vary depending on factors such as size, color, and texture. Some common grades include fancy, choice, standard, and substandard, with fancy being the highest quality and substandard being the lowest quality.
